_Last updated: 23 July 2026_

AWSD is committed to protecting your privacy. This page describes the data we collect and
how it is used, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## Data controller

The data controller is **AWSD** (Jean-Vivien Sicot).
Contact: [jeanvivien.sicot@lapiscine.pro](mailto:jeanvivien.sicot@lapiscine.pro).

## Data we collect

This is a showcase website: **it has no forms, no user accounts and no advertising cookies**.

- **Email contact** — When you write to us, we process your email address and the content
  of your message for the sole purpose of replying to you. Legal basis: your request
  (pre-contractual measures / legitimate interest). These exchanges are never sold or
  shared with third parties for marketing purposes.
- **Audience measurement** — As it stands, the site **uses no third-party analytics or
  advertising trackers**. No browsing data that could identify you is collected.

## Cookies

No tracking or advertising cookies are set. Only browser local storage (`localStorage`) is
used to remember your light/dark theme preference; this information stays on your device
and is never transmitted.

## Retention period

Emails exchanged when you get in touch are kept for as long as necessary to handle your
request, then archived or deleted.

## Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict and object to the
processing of your data. To exercise these rights, write to
[jeanvivien.sicot@lapiscine.pro](mailto:jeanvivien.sicot@lapiscine.pro). You may also lodge
a complaint with the French data protection authority, the [CNIL](https://www.cnil.fr).

## Hosting

Technical data (server logs) is processed by the host **Clever Cloud SAS**, whose servers
are located in the European Union.
